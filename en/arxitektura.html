Architecture

Interior architecture - is the design of a building or shelter from the inside, or a new interior design for a type of home that can be renovated. This may refer to the original design and plan used for the interior of a building, to recreate an interior made to meet a modified purpose, or to revise the original design for adaptive reuse of the shell of the corresponding building. the latter is often part of the practice of sustainable architecture, so resources are provided by "processing" the structure through adaptive processing.

In general, environmental design is called spatial art, interior architecture also refers to a process that is designed to address all aspects of human use of their structural spaces inside buildings. Simply put, interior architecture is interior design from an architectural point of view.
